FAQ

How do I pay the order?

I accept payment via bank transfer or deposit account.

How can I check prices?

Prices vary depending on the time it takes me to do each design, the beads used in it and the extras.
For example, a design of about 1/2 hour and 400 beads costs € 3.50 plus shipping costs.
The same design with an extra magnet to put it in the fridge would be € 4.50 (+ shipping costs).

What extras are available for the designs?

  • Brooch for clothing (small or large)
  • No closing hook earrings
  • Earrings with clip
  • Phone string
  • Fridge magnet
  • Key chain

If you are interested in anything else, as a necklace, a safety pin for a jacket, send an email with your request :)

Do you do deliveries outside of Spain?

No, just use regular mail Post Service.
However if you are interested and live outside, you can always order from a friend or relative who resides in Spain.

How are the orders submited?

Orders are shipped in a padded envelope, by post.

How long does it take to get my order home?

If your order is in stock, it will take 7 to 10 business days. Remember that this will depend on the postal service.

I can cancel or modify an order?

Yes, if it is not already packaged or has been shipped.

If I send you my design would you make it into beads?

Yes, considering that not all colors are available in Hama Beads and I may have to make color adjustments. Also keep in mind that if you order a large design delivery may be delayed.

About the beads ...

The beads, are small plastic hollow cylinders. They are placed on a plastic pegboard forming designs.
So each bead is like a pixel!

Once the design is ready, a special paper is placed over the beads and pressed with an iron, to melt the plastic, the design is fixed, et voila, we have our design permanently attached :)


There are several brands of beads, Hama (Danish) is the best known brand, there are also two Swedish brands, Nabi and Photopearls.
The main difference is the colors offered by each brand.
I use Hama Beads.


There are 3 bead sizes of the brand Hama

  • Mini: to work with them, you'll need a pin, good eyesight and a steady hand
  • Midi: they are approximately 1cm high and and easier to use than the mini
  • Maxi: this size of beads is designed for children

For Midi there are some more shades: gold, silver, bronze, yellow (Winnie the Pooh) and glow in the dark in various colors.

The colors of the Hama Beads are a total of 48, both as Hama Beads Mini Hama Beads Midi.
